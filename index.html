How to Play

The Goal

Be the first to complete a row, column, or diagonal (5 in a row).

How It Works

  • Enter your name to get your unique randomised card.
  • When you spot something happening in real life, tap the square.
  • You'll be asked for an optional note (who said it, what session) — for validation.
  • The centre square is free — because someone will definitely say "So..."

Winning

  • When you get a line, the card will prompt you to email Silviu with your claim.
  • The group validates your 5 squares — timestamps + notes are your evidence.
  • If a square is rejected, play continues.

Bonus: Blackout

  • After someone wins a line, keep playing until someone fills the whole card.
  • No self-triggering: you can't say something yourself to fill your own square.
