How to Play

The Goal

Be the first to complete a row, column, or diagonal (5 in a row).

How It Works

  • Enter your name to get your unique randomised card.
  • When you spot something happening, tap the square.
  • You'll be prompted to fill in who said it, which session, and roughly when — this is required for validation.
  • The centre square is free and always checked.

Winning

  • When you get BINGO, take a screenshot of your card + activity log (scroll down on mobile).
  • Post it in the team Slack channel with "BINGO!" 🎉
  • The group validates your squares — the activity log shows who, when, and which session for each claim.
  • If a square is rejected, play continues.

Bonus: Blackout

  • After someone wins a line, keep playing for full card blackout.
  • No self-triggering: you can't say something yourself to fill your own square.
