How to Play

The Goal

Be the first to complete a row, column, or diagonal (5 in a row).

How It Works

  • When you spot something on the card happening in real life, tap the square.
  • You'll be asked for an optional note (who said it, what session, etc.) — this is for validation later.
  • The centre square is free — because someone will definitely say "So..."
  • Your card layout is randomised — everyone has the same squares in a different order, so it's a fair race.

Validation

  • When someone shouts "BINGO!", the group validates their 5 squares.
  • Each square has a timestamp and optional note in the activity log.
  • The group votes: does each claim count? Majority rules.
  • If a square is rejected, play continues.

Bonus Rules (optional)

  • Blackout: After someone wins a line, keep playing until someone fills the whole card.
  • No self-triggering: You can't say something yourself to fill your own square.
  • Double points: If the same person triggers 3+ of your squares, that's a bonus.
